A Little About Me

A Passionate Data Analyst

Once I left high school, I felt as though I knew where my life was going. My plan was to go to trade school and become a licensed aesthetician (skin care professional). I quickly learned just how much that career path was not suited to me as an individual. Instead, I chose to do a massive pivot and pursue a career in STEM. I enrolled in a computer science program and initially began with software engineering, but soon discovered my passion for working with data. I loved it so much, I decided to get a Master's degree too. As someone who is naturally inquisitive, working with data is my cup of tea. I just love seeing how a multitude of different factors can come together to paint a bigger picture. Our world thrives and evolves using the we data collect, and never before has data been collected in such massive quantities at the break-neck pace we see today. The world is in dire need of talented professionals who know how to transform said data into actionable and understandable formats. I hope to cement myself as one of those capable individuals.
